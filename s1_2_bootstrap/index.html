A Simple Bookmark Manager

A clean and simple interface to organize your favourite websites. Open a new browser tab and see your sites load instantly. Try it for free.

Features

Our aim is to make it quick and easy for you to access your favorite websites. Your bookmarks sync between your devices and browsers, so you can access them anywhere you wish.

Bookmark in one click

Organize your bookmarks hovewer you like. Our simple drag-and-drop interface gives you complete control over how you manage your favorite sites.

Intelligent search

Our powerful search feature will help you find saved sites in no time at all. No need to trawl through all of your bookmarks.

Sharing your bookmarks

Easily share your bookmarks and collections with others. Create a shareable link that you can send at the click of botton.
